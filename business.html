Business in Fortville

Fortville has a thriving business community, contributing to the town's economic vitality. Here's an overview of our local businesses and opportunities.

Local Businesses

Fortville is home to a diverse range of businesses, to name a few, there is a gun store that offers many options for self-defense or weapons for those who appreciate the craftsmanship. They have handguns, rifles, shotguns, and a gun range to test them before purchase. The town also has a couple of ice cream shops that are very accommodating, offering dairy-free alternatives for those with dietary restrictions or preferences. There are also a few car repair shops in town that do an amazing service for a fair price and are honest, which is hard to come by these days.

Fortville, a rapidly growing city, boasts an increasing number of local businesses. Broadway and Main Street serve as the primary areas, offering a diverse range of shops and restaurants. The town actively encourages the establishment of new businesses, ensuring its residents have a variety of options. As Fortville expands, so do the employment opportunities.   • Amazon
  • Walmart
  • Hancock Health
  • Mt. Vernon Community School Corporation

Economic Opportunities

Fortville offers an ideal environment for new businesses to thrive and entrepreneurs to capitalize on its strategic location within Hancock County. It also has proximity to major transportation routes, which provide the businesses and vendors with access to a much wider customer base. The presence of significant employers in the surrounding area, such as Amazon, Walmart, Hancock Health, and Mt. Vernon Community School Corporation, indicates a strong and available workforce.

To add to that, the town encourages the development of new businesses with a welcoming atmosphere for startups and expansions. Both Broadway and Main St provide established locations with pre-existing foot and vehicle traffic. With this combination of location, workforce, and supportive government, makes Fortville an attractive place to invest and build a business.

Commerce and Development

The town is actively engaged in the revitalization of its historic downtown area. Current initiatives include facade improvement programs for local businesses and infrastructure upgrades to enhance walkability and attract more visitors to Broadway and Main Street. These efforts aim to preserve Fortville's unique charm while creating a more vibrant and appealing commercial center for both residents and entrepreneurs.
