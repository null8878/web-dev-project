About Our Town

Fortville is more than just a dot on the map it's a vibrant community steeped in rich history and Hoosier hospitality. From its early railroad roots to the charming character of its preserved downtown, Fortville offers both nostalgia and growth. Learn how our town evolved, meet the people who shaped it, and explore what continues to make Fortville one of Indiana’s best-kept secrets.

Living in Fortville

Whether you're a young professional, a growing family, or looking for a place to retire, Fortville has something for everyone. Visiting Fortville

Fortville is a hidden gem for travelers who love discovering local charm. Take a stroll through our vibrant downtown, filled with boutique shops, bakeries, and cozy cafés. Seasonal festivals, family-friendly parks, and a welcoming community vibe make Fortville a destination that’s both relaxing and exciting.
